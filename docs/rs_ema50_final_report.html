Methodology & Assumptions

Entry signalRS ratio (Stock Close ÷ Nifty 50 Close) EMA50 crosses above EMA200
Exit signalRS EMA50 crosses below EMA200 (symmetric), OR price hits 10% hard stop from entry
ExecutionSignal computed at day's close; order filled at next day's open; unfilled orders expire after 1 day
Position sizingFixed risk: Capital × 0.5% ÷ 10% = ₹50,000 notional per trade
Universe filterMean daily turnover ≥ ₹5 crore across the sim period (429 stocks qualify)
CapacityMaximum 15 concurrent positions; new signals queue and fill as slots free up
Data sourceDaily OHLCV, auto-adjusted for splits/dividends
Not modelledBrokerage, STT, slippage, impact cost. Returns are gross of transaction costs (tax IS modelled separately above)
Caveats for the desk: (1) Backtest assumes perfect next-open fills with no slippage — real fills on 15 concurrent swing positions will incur some cost. (2) The 24% drawdown is real and must be tolerable to capture the 24% CAGR. (3) Survivorship: universe is current NSE liquid names; delisted/merged stocks are not included. (4) 2021–2025 was a broadly bullish regime for Indian equities — forward returns depend on regime.
